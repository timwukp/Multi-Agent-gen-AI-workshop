Amazon Business Overview 2025

Company Profile:
Amazon.com, Inc. is a multinational technology company focusing on e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence.

Core Business Segments:

1. E-commerce and Retail
- Amazon.com marketplace
- Prime membership program (over 200 million members globally)
- Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA)
- Amazon Fresh and Whole Foods Market

2. Amazon Web Services (AWS)
- Leading cloud computing platform
- Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS)
- Platform as a Service (PaaS)
- Software as a Service (SaaS)
- Machine Learning and AI services

3. Digital Content and Advertising
- Prime Video streaming service
- Amazon Music
- Kindle and Audible
- Amazon Advertising platform

4. Devices and Services
- Echo smart speakers and Alexa
- Fire tablets and TV devices
- Ring security products
- Amazon Pharmacy

Key Competitive Advantages:
- Customer obsession culture
- Vast logistics and fulfillment network
- Technology innovation and R&D investment
- Prime ecosystem lock-in effect
- Data-driven decision making

Market Position:
- #1 in cloud computing (AWS)
- #2 in global e-commerce
- Growing presence in digital advertising
- Expanding international markets
